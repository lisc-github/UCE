Teach in China

 

You are wondering whether you should go to China and teach and, if you go, how you will grow and change and what benefits you will derive. Here are some observations.

 

"One half year or a year in China will give you the opportunity to teach some bright and dedicated students. You will form profound and long lasting friendships with colleagues and students. It will give you the chance to travel to the most amazingly beautiful and geographically and culturally diverse places in the world, not as a tourist, but as an insider-someone who is living and working in the country. It will give you insight into the oldest, most populated, culturally rich and complex society on earth. You will observe and participate in the fastest growing economy of any nation in the world today. You will also witness poverty, bureaucratic suffocation, and crowding of terrifying proportions. You will learn to love the country so much that at times you may hate it with a passion, but you will always want to go back".

 

UCE not only offers English teaching opportunities in China, but also highly desired professional teaching opportunities in varies subjects to those with teaching experience as well as those who are not currently teachers but working in the industries of related subjects.

Teach English in China

Working as an English teacher in a foreign country for one year or two provides you a great way to transit from college to a professional career. Our Teaching English in China program is for any qualified individual who is looking for a meaningful teaching and learning experience as short as one semester in China. In addition to a well-paid teaching position (including paid vacation and public holidays), you’ll also receive extensive English teaching resources and material, teacher training, visa support and lifetime job referral service. Our partner schools are spread throughout mainland China, many of which located in scenery cities with rich cultural and natural heritage.

The Center also provides free Chinese Language & Culture lessons, guided tour to historic attractions for participants who also join TESL Summer Camp program.

The placements are for English language teachers. To be considered for half year or a full year placement, applicants must be a native English speaker with love of teaching. You do not need to know any Chinese or have taught previously. You must be comfortable speaking in front of groups, be enthusiastic about teaching, and have strong organizational skill. You must take your 'ambassadorial role' seriously, as you will be highly regarded in your local community and will be seen as our Center’s representative at host institution.

* This program may qualify for TESL/TEFL internship and/or up to 10 credit hours accepted by colleges and universities (you need pre-approval from your home institution).

Faculty Sabbatical Program

UCE also helps to arrange joint research projects in related fields with Chinese counterparties if the purpose is to conduct research. Please see RESEARCH for detail.
