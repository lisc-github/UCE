US-CHINA Education and Culture Exchange Center (US-China Edu), a non-political, non-religious, non-profit organization committed to develop a needed symbiosis between two nations separated by 10,000 miles. US-China Edu's mission is to foster cross-cultural understanding between Chinese and Americans. The organization achieves this goal by providing Americans and Chinese with various educational opportunities to study, work and live in US or China.

All teaching positions are paid with international tickets covered and accommodation provided for a small amount of program fee. Various contract lengths are available which provide you with great flexibility.. No Chinese language ability required.

Teach, Live and Experience China - Immerse in the Chinese Culture and Way of life, whether you're a recent college graduate, a teacher, a professor or someone working in a professional field. Be a part of this rewarding, enjoyable and unforgettable experience of a lifetime.

Paid Teaching Positions

UCE provides various teaching opportunities, including teaching English (no prior teaching experience required), experienced and professional teaching positions, and sabbatical program for as short as 2 weeks.

Study in China

UCE helps qualified students to obtain enrollment to Chinese universities. Scholarships are guaranteed for programs of various lengths, whether you apply to study for a major or to study Chinese.

TESL Summer Camp

Earn TESL Certificate while teaching English and travelling in China. The Program, which is available in 4, 5, 7, 8 and 12-week lengths, may be qualified for college credits.

Application Process

The application process is simple -- all you need is have your passport and resume ready, then fill out the online application form. UCE staff will guide you through the whole visa process as well.
